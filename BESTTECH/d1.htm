      About Our Doctor

      Dr Deepali Rathod is a Senior Clinical physiotherapist and certified Mulligan Practitioner (CMP) with more than 7 years of Experience. She has profound skill in Pre and post joint replacement, Spinal decompression and discectomy, Commando surgeries, Cardiovascular and respiratory rehabilitation conditions. She is well-respected and an important member of the Physiotherapy fraternity who is well equipped with administrative and clinical responsibilities. Her main motto is preventing pain and deformity.
      She helps her patients to regain their lives by rehabilitation program
      Her Area of expertise lies in pre and post joint replacement rehabilitation. She is known for her expertise in Lower back (spondylosis, spondylitis, spondylolisthesis)
      Cervical spondylosis with chronic headache.

      Special interests

      • Function training
      • Marathon training
      • Cardio vascular (Post CABG) Rehabilitation
      • Pre and postnatal exercises (Pregnancy and gynae care)
